## v1.0 Release

Keep your games moving with zChess Clock, a clean, chess clock that’s easy to use and fully customizable. Whether you’re blitzing with friends or testing long time controls, ChessClock has you covered.

### Features

- Classic and modern modes – choose from standard time controls or create your own.
- Custom timers – add increment, delay, or Bronstein time delay.
- Hourglass mode – watch time flow between players for a fast, dynamic feel.
- Simple and stylish design – tap to switch turns, and focus on the fun.
- Save your favorites – quickly load your most-used setups.

Built for chess lovers who just want to hit play and start the clock.

Ready? Tap, move, and let the games begin.
